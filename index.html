We offer much more than just teaching.

Our language lessons are based on interactive and dynamic speaking exercises digital tools. Our experienced language consultants create a customized course program that motivates and inspires. You can choose to learn a language in groups or individually and on-site or by distance. No matter what you chose, our goal is to provide fun, interesting and challenging lessons for you.

We want you to grow your language and your communication.

GROUP COURSES

Our group courses focus on developing conversational skills and practical understanding of Swedish at different levels. We develop communication skills by activating the new language in everyday situations. Our teachers ensure creative and dynamic classes designed to increase participants’ confidence and joy of speaking and understanding the local language. All course programs include free social and cultural activities aimed at connecting internationals with locals.

INDIVIDUAL COURSES

Private lessons is the most motivating and efficient way to learn a language. We offer a tailor-made solution to learn the new language as quickly as possible and on your terms. The course content is defined by the participant’s personal learning objectives and needs, ensuring fast and efficient progress. We ensure individual scheduling and location selected by the learner. We are able to come to your workplace or home, or hold the lessons at our premises if preferred.

ON-LINE COURSES

If learning online and by distance is most convenient for you we offer individual lessons via Skype with professional native Swedish teachers at all levels. Each course is designed to your learning styles and preferences. Our teacher ensures an engaging and interactive course program and will meet you online everywhere you want. No advanced aids needed, standard internet connection and a communication media is enough.
